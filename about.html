About Us

Who We Are

Rochester's most unique dessert experience

Sweet Retreat isn't just a dessert shop — it's a destination. From the moment you walk through our doors, you're greeted like family, guided through a menu built to impress, and treated to desserts that genuinely can't be found anywhere else in Rochester.

Our Story

Built on passion, crafted with care

Sweet Retreat was born from a simple belief: Rochester deserved something extraordinary. We set out to create a space where every detail matters — from the ingredients we source to the way we welcome every guest who walks in.

Every item on our menu is made fresh to order. We don't cut corners. Whether it's our Dubai Strawberry Cups piled high with fresh fruit and pistachio kunafa, our golden bubble waffles, or our handcrafted artisan crepes, everything is prepared with the same love and attention every single time.

Our team is what makes Sweet Retreat truly special. We train every person not just to serve food, but to create an experience — one worth sharing, one worth coming back for. And our guests agree. With a perfect 5-star rating and over 100 glowing reviews, we're proud to have made Rochester a little sweeter.
